Privacy Policy

Your data is yours.

DoneBuild handles business, client, estimate, invoice, and payment workflow data. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it. Last updated May 2026.

1. Information we collect

We may collect your name, email, phone number, company information, account details, usage activity, project details, estimates, invoices, change orders, client names, and client contact details that you enter into DoneBuild.

2. Payment data

Payment information is handled by Stripe. DoneBuild does not store full card numbers or bank credentials.

3. How we use information

We use information to operate DoneBuild, send estimates and invoices on your behalf, support your account, improve the product, maintain security, and communicate important service updates.

4. What we do not do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your project or client data with advertisers. We do not access your job information for support unless it is needed to help you or keep the service working.

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and analytics to understand website and product usage, improve reliability, and make DoneBuild easier to use.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including reputable cloud infrastructure and access controls, to protect account and business data.

7. Your choices

You can request access, correction, deletion, export, or cancellation by emailing tommy@donebuild.com.

8. Updates

We may update this policy as DoneBuild evolves. If the change is significant, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users.
