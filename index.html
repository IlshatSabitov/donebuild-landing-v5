Questions you'd ask Tommy at the bar

Honest answers.

Is this just ChatGPT in a wrapper?

No. DoneBuild is a custom estimating engine that runs on your accepted jobs plus ZIP-level material and labor data. Nothing about your pricing leaves your account, and nothing in your estimates trains anyone else's model. The more jobs you run through it, the more it sounds like you wrote it. You own the data. Leave any time, take it with you.

Will I have to learn new software?

No. If you can leave a voicemail, you can use DoneBuild. First-time users send their first signed estimate in under 90 seconds. No onboarding call. No manual. No YouTube tutorials. If you get stuck, text the founder.

Do I have to use voice?

No. Build any estimate by hand. Voice is just the fast path. Manual estimating is always one click away. Most users stop reaching for it after job three, but it's there forever.

What's the catch with $79/month?

You're one of the first 100 users helping prove this out. In exchange you get the price for life. After 100 users it goes to $129/mo. Your $79 doesn't move, even when the public price doubles. No tier games. No "but only on annual." No asterisks.

Will the voice mishear me on a noisy jobsite?

It handles ambient noise well (saws, music, traffic) and ignores filler words automatically. If a line item gets garbled, you can edit it inline before sending. Every transcript is logged so you can audit anything.

Where do the prices come from?

Your saved unit rates plus ZIP-level material pricing pulled from contractor supply networks. The first time you run an estimate, you confirm your defaults. After that, prices match your actual jobs. Nothing changes silently.

Do my clients need an account or app?

No. They click a link, review, sign, and pay. No password, no download. Works on any phone or laptop browser.

Does this replace QuickBooks?

No. Keep QuickBooks for accounting. DoneBuild handles estimate, approval, change order, invoice, paid. QuickBooks sync is on the roadmap.

Is this really built for solo operators?

Yes. The whole product is shaped by feedback from solo and 1 to 5 person kitchen and bath crews. No bloat. No project-management theatrics. If you're running 50+ jobs a year, you'll outgrow it. That's fine.

What if it doesn't work for me?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions. That's enough time to run 1 to 3 real jobs through it. If it doesn't pay for itself in your first job, you shouldn't be paying for it.

What's not in DoneBuild yet?

QuickBooks sync is in build. SMS delivery is next. Margin and profit tracking, selections and allowances, in-app notifications, and follow-ups are in design. Founding 100 ships first. If something you need isn't on the list, text Tommy. Odds are it's already on the wall, just not yet on the page.

Have a crew or a showroom?

The crew tier is being built right now with a small group of founding showroom partners. Locked-in pricing and the same direct line to the founder for the first ones in. Text Tommy if that's you.
