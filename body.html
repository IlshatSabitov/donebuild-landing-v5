Questions you'd ask Tommy at the bar

Honest answers.

I'm not high-tech. Will I be able to use this?

If you can send text messages on your phone, you can use DoneBuild. The product is built for guys with sawdust on their phone screen, not guys hiding behind a desk. Most contractors send their first signed estimate without watching a single tutorial.

I only do 1 to 2 jobs at a time. Am I too small?

No. Solo operators running 1 to 5 jobs at a time are exactly who this was built for.

I do mostly bathroom remodels, not full kitchens. Does that matter?

No. Kitchen and bath share the same workflow shape: spec materials, get approval, handle change orders, collect payment. Half baths, master suites, small kitchen refreshes, and full gut remodels all fit the same DoneBuild flow.

I have a partner or one helper. Does it work for 2 people?

Yes. The founding price is per account, not per seat. If you and your partner share one DoneBuild account, that is $79/mo total. Multi-user accounts with separate logins are planned for the crew tier.

Do I have to use voice?

No. Build any estimate by hand. Voice is just the fast path. Manual estimating is always one click away.

Can I edit the estimate after the voice draft?

Yes. Voice creates the draft. You can edit any line, including quantities, prices, descriptions, and notes, before sending. Voice gets you most of the way there fast. The final judgment is still yours.

What if a client wants a paper estimate or a PDF for their records?

They get both. Clients can review and sign online, and every estimate can travel as a PDF for email, printing, or filing.

Can I customize the estimate to match my brand?

Yes. Your logo, business name, license number, and company address can sit on estimates, change orders, and invoices. Add it once, then it carries through the workflow.

I already use QuickBooks. Will DoneBuild conflict?

No. DoneBuild handles what happens before the deposit hits: estimates, change orders, signed approvals, invoices, and payment collection. QuickBooks handles your books after the money lands. QuickBooks sync is in build.

Where do the prices come from?

Your saved unit rates, accepted job history, and ZIP-level material context. You confirm your defaults. Nothing changes silently.

Will the voice mishear me on a noisy jobsite?

It handles normal jobsite noise well, and every estimate is editable before it goes to the client. If a line item is wrong, fix it before sending.

Do my clients need an account or app?

No. They click a link, review, sign, and pay. No password, no download. It works on any phone or laptop browser.

What if I sign up and don't have time to use it?

You get a 14-day free trial. If life gets busy and you do not get a real job through it in time, text Tommy and ask for help. The point is to prove it on a real estimate, not trap you in a bill.

What if I make a mistake on an estimate after sending it?

You can update an unsigned estimate and send a corrected version. If it is already signed, send a change order with the correction. Same workflow you would use for any scope change.

I don't want a salesperson calling me. Is signing up going to put me on a list?

No drip campaign, no sales floor, no random call center. The only human you should hear from is Tommy or the DoneBuild team helping you get value from the product.

What's not in DoneBuild yet?

QuickBooks sync is in build. SMS delivery is next. Margin and profit tracking, selections and allowances, in-app notifications, and follow-ups are in design. Founding users shape what ships first.
