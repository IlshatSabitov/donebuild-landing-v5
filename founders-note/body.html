Founder note

Built for the remodeler doing the work and running the business.

DoneBuild exists because the hardest part of a kitchen or bath job is often everything around the work: estimates, approvals, change orders, invoices, payments, and the admin cleanup after a long day.

Why we built it

I kept hearing the same story from small remodeling crews: the work is not the problem. The paperwork is. A solo remodeler can build a beautiful kitchen, then lose the night to Word docs, spreadsheets, text-message change orders, unpaid invoices, and tools that were never built for how they actually work.

Big companies can absorb complicated software. A solo kitchen and bath remodeler cannot spend ten hours learning a system before sending the next estimate.

The workflow we care about

DoneBuild is focused on one workflow: estimate, approval, change order, invoice, payment. Voice estimating is the fast path, but the bigger promise is simpler than that. One clean place to price the job, get the client to sign, handle scope changes, and collect money without chasing five apps.

Built with the early crews

If you are one of the early remodelers using DoneBuild, thank you. Your feedback decides what gets sharper next. We are building this with solo and small kitchen and bath crews, not for a generic contractor market.

Tommy, founder of DoneBuild
